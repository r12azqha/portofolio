The Hidden 'R'

At its core, it's just raw geometry. But the negative space aggressively carves out a hidden 'r' for my name. It's about building structure out of the void—creating something undeniably brutalist yet entirely mine.

The Viewport

I spend my life inside 3D software. That harsh outer bracket is my viewport. It's the literal frame through which I execute my ideas, anchoring the chaos of the creative process into focused reality.

Duality

Look at the tension between the rigid angles and the sweeping internal curve. It mirrors how I operate—pushing the absolute technical limits of 3D modeling while keeping the creative flow completely unconstrained.
