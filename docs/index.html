Primary Mark

Visual * Meaning

Concept

The primary mark is a brutalist abstraction of the letter 'R'. Relying entirely on raw geometry and negative space, it establishes a bold, dominant structural presence.

Symbol

The rigid outer bracket acts as the 'viewport'—the literal frame through which I execute my work in 3D software. It serves to anchor the chaotic, unpredictable nature of the creative process into a focused, highly controlled reality.

Typography

The typography strips away unnecessary hierarchy to focus strictly on raw information. The stark italicization forces a heavy forward momentum, acting as a visual counter-balance to the static, geometric weight of the primary mark.

Meaning

The tension between the sharp exterior angles and the sweeping internal negative space mirrors my workflow: pushing the absolute technical limits of 3D modeling while keeping the creative flow unconstrained.
