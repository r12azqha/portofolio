About Me

Hi, I'm Raja Razqha Sutan Rabani — a Game Design student with a strong interest in 3D Art and becoming a professional 3D Artist.

Through my studies, I've explored various areas such as branding, illustration, animation, and 3D modeling, which allow me to understand design from multiple perspectives.

I aim to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also meaningful - designs that connect ideas with emotions.

The Viewport

The harsh outer bracket mimics a camera frame or a 3D software viewport. It represents my perspective and lens as a 3D artist, framing the subject with precision.

Duality of Form

The contrast between the rigid 90-degree outer angles and the smooth internal curve represents the intersection of technical execution (hard-surface modeling) and creative fluidity.

The Hidden 'r'

Using aggressive negative space, the geometry subtly constructs an abstract lowercase 'r' for "r12azqha". It's structural, heavy, and leaves an immediate brutalist impression.
